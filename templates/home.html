The Process

Three Steps to Clear Communication

From camera to speech in seconds — no special hardware required.

1
Open Camera

Grant camera permission in your browser. Works on any laptop or mobile device — no app download needed.

2
Perform a Sign

Position your hands clearly in frame and make any sign. SignBridge captures a frame every 3 seconds automatically.

3
AI Analyzes

Google Gemini Vision AI examines the hand gesture, identifies the sign, and returns a translated phrase with a confidence score.

4
Spoken Aloud

The translated text is instantly read aloud using your device's text-to-speech engine — enabling real two-way communication.

Features

Built for Real Conversations

Every feature is designed to make communication smoother, faster, and more accessible.

Camera-First Design

Opens directly in the browser. No plugins, no installation. Works with laptop webcams and phone cameras equally well.

Real-Time Detection

Auto-capture mode analyzes your gestures every 3 seconds. Manual capture also available for precise control.

Instant Voice Output

Translations are spoken aloud immediately using the Web Speech API — no server round-trip needed for audio.

Multi-Language Support

Supports ASL, BSL, KSL (Kenyan), AUSLAN, and International Sign, with more being added continuously.

Confidence Scoring

Every translation shows an AI confidence percentage so you always know how reliable the result is.

Feedback Loop

Rate translations and submit corrections. Your feedback helps improve AI accuracy for everyone.
